About The Marigold Manor

Welcome to The Marigold Manor, your perfect venue for weddings, parties, corporate events, and special celebrations. Established in 2001, we have been providing exceptional services and a beautiful space to create unforgettable memories.

Our hall offers spacious rooms, a large garden, and ample parking to accommodate your guests comfortably. We pride ourselves on our excellent customer service, attention to detail, and commitment to making every event a success.

Whether you are planning an intimate gathering or a grand celebration, our team is here to help you every step of the way. We also provide modern amenities, reliable security, and customized event packages tailored to your needs.

Thank you for considering The Marigold Manor. We look forward to hosting your next special event!
